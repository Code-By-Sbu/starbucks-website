STARBUCKS


Starbucks Corporation is an American multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery reserves headquartered in Seattle, Washington. It was founded in 1971, and is currently the world's largest coffeehouse chain. Starbucks has 35,711 stores in 80 countries, in South Africa we currently have 54 stores.

Service The rise of coffee culture is generally attributed to Starbucks, serving hot drinks like cappuccinos and cold drinks like iced-latte to customers who range from coffee addicts to first time drinkers of the famous coffee. Starbucks offers space to have meetings by providing free Wi-Fi, coffee dates or hanging out with friends enjoying different drinks coupled with pastries (sandwiches, muffins, bagels) and in a morning rush you have the option of heading to the drive through to place your breakfast order. Starbucks prides itself by providing personalized experience, rewarding customers by launching a loyalty program for registered users of the Starbucks Card offering perks and discounts and offerings non-dairy milk to lactose intolerant customers.

Products Starbucks has a merchandise available for purchase, ensuring that you can have the Starbucks experience at home. Products you can take home for your personal coffee station are as follows:
• Whole-bean coffee • Instant coffee • Coffee pods • Tumblers • Coffee making instruments • Bottles Low calorie products are on offer for health-conscious customers and every year Starbucks releases new holiday merchandise, including new paper cups with various festive designs.
